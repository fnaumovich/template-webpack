Questions and Answers

Can I redeem two or more drinks under the Coffee Subscription at the same time? Is there any limit?
There is no limit under the Coffee Subscription. You can even redeem all drinks at once; they just need to be redeemed one by one.
 
What size of a drink is available under CoffeeMap subscription?
Size of a drink depends on the Coffee Shop. Most of our partner Coffee Shops offer drinks of a standard size, some of them offer large size drinks. Please ask barista at the Coffee Shop.
What drinks are included into «Black» Ð¸ «White» Coffee Subscriptions?

Under «Black» subscription you can enjoy any black coffee of your choice: Espresso, Americano, Ristretto. To stay or to go. At some Coffee Shops – alternative coffee brewing methods are available.

Under «White» subscription you can enjoy any drink of your choice, with milk or without. Cappuccino, Latte, Raf, Hot Chocolate, Cacao, Tea, Smoothie and other drinks. To stay or to go. Also includes any drink from «Black» subscription.

What should I do if my mobile device does not show your service correctly?
Our service works correctly on the following software: Android 5.1 and later versions (Browsers: Firefox 40+, Chrome 44+, IE 10+, Safari 9), IOS 8.4.1and later verions. If the version of your software IOS is earlier than version 8.4.1 – please update it, otherwise we will not be able to guarantee the correct service.
How do I know what coffee shops near me are CoffeeMap partners?
Our interactive map shows location of all our partner coffee shops. No matter where you are, all you need to do is just open the mobile app or mobile version of the website and you will see where you can enjoy great coffee under CoffeeMap subscription.
Am I to use the Coffee Subscription only at one partner CoffeeShop?
Absolutely no! This is the beauty of CoffeeMap. You have an opportunity to try a new drink every day, either at different places or at your favorite Coffee Shop. You are to choose!
What if my GPS is off and I do not want you to know my location?
No problems! In case your GPS is off, you will see the Coffee Shops listed in the alphabetical order. Although it is not such fun, don’t you think?
Why do you need the details of my accounts in social networks and what do you do with them?
Signing in via Facebook account makes the registration easier. Also it is a simple way to share with your friends. And please, do not worry! We are not using your details for marketing purposes, and we shall never publish anything on your behalf.
What do I need to use CoffeeMap?
You only need a smartphone and Internet!
